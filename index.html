About Us

We are a team of local youth who are striving to provide awareness of the Quran to the London community. Our goal is to raise awareness of the Quran through online broadcasting.

Mission

Al-Quran Radio is an online radio streaming channel that promotes Quran recitation and teachings of the Quran ongoing 24/7. Being one of the first of its kind in Canada, and especially in London, Ontario, Al-Quran radio is promoting a positive atmosphere to all communities and cities across Canada.

We Offer

Variety of Quranic reciters | Jummah Khutbahs from Muslim institutions in London Ontario such as London Muslim Mosque and Islamic Center | Segments from London's very own Muslim scholars and teachers | Timely Athan
